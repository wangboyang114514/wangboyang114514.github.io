镄 (Fm)

危险类别：无特殊危害信息

分子式：Fm

分子量：257.0 g/mol

CAS号：无CAS号

同义词：无同义词

创建日期：2024-01-01

描述

Radioactive 金属 transuranic element, belongs to the actinoids. Ten known isotopes, most stable is Fm-257 with a half-life of 10 days. First 鉴定 由 Albert Ghiorso and associates in the debris of the first 氢-bomb explosion in 1952.
