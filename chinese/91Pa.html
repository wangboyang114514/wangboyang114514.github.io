镤 (Pa)

危险类别：无特殊危害信息

分子式：Pa

分子量：231.03588 g/mol

CAS号：无CAS号

同义词：无同义词

创建日期：2024-01-01

描述

Radioactive 金属 element, belongs to the actinoids. The most stable isotope, Pa-231 has a half-life of 2.43*10^4 years. At least 10 other 放射性同位素 are known. No practical applications are known. Discovered in 1917 由 Lise Meitner and Otto Hahn.
