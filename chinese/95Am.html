描述

Radioactive 金属 transuranic element, belongs to the actinoids. Ten known isotopes. Am-243 is the most stable isotope, with a half-life of 7.95*10^3 years. Discovered 由 Glenn T. Seaborg and associates in 1945, it was obtained 由 bombarding Uranium-238 with alpha particles.
