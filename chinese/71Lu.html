镥 (Lu)

危险类别：无特殊危害信息

分子式：Lu

分子量：174.9668 g/mol

CAS号：无CAS号

同义词：无同义词

创建日期：2024-01-01

描述

Silvery-白色 rare-earth metal which is relatively stable in air. It happens to be the most expensive rare-earth metal. Its found with almost all rare-earth metals, but is very difficult to separate from other elements. Least abundant of all natural elements. Used in metal alloys, and as a catalyst in various processes. 有两种 natural, 稳定同位素, and seven radioisotopes, the most stable being Lu-174 with a half-life of 3.3 years. The separation of lutetium from Ytterbium was described 由 Georges Urbain in 1907. It was 发现 at approximately the same time 由 Carl Auer von Welsbach. The name comes from the Greek word lutetia which means Paris.
