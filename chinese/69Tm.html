铥 (Tm)

危险类别：无特殊危害信息

分子式：Tm

分子量：168.93422 g/mol

CAS号：无CAS号

同义词：无同义词

创建日期：2024-01-01

描述

Soft grey 金属 element that belongs to the lanthanoids. One natural isotope exists, Tm-169, and seventeen artificial isotopes have been produced. No known uses for the element. Discovered in 1879 由 Per Theodor Cleve.
