铋 (Bi)

危险类别：无特殊危害信息

分子式：Bi

分子量：208.9804 g/mol

CAS号：无CAS号

同义词：无同义词

创建日期：2024-01-01

描述

White crystalline metal with a pink tinge, belongs to 族 15. Most diamagnetic of all metals and has the lowest thermal conductivity of all the elements except mercury. Lead-free bismuth 化合物 are used in cosmetics and medical procedures. Burns in the air and produces a blue flame. In 1753, C.G. Junine first demonstrated that it was different from lead.
