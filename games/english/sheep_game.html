Game Instructions

Game Rules:

1. Click on elements in the game area to place them in the slots below

2. When you have three identical or related elements in the slots, they will be eliminated

3. You can have a maximum of 7 elements in the slots

4. The goal is to eliminate all element blocks

5. Each elimination earns 10 points, with bonus points for consecutive eliminations

Learning Features:

- The game displays element symbols, names, atomic numbers, and other basic information

- After completing the game, you will see the chemistry knowledge you learned during gameplay

- You can deepen your understanding of the periodic table through gameplay
