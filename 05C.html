Description Boron is a black or brown substance that exists in many crystalline or amorphous forms. It is not reactive at room temperature, but it becomes more reactive at high temperatures. Boron is an essential nutrient for plants, and it is used in various industries for making borosilicate glass, ceramics, fertilizers, and detergents. It has semiconductor properties that are valuable in electronic devices.
